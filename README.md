🎓 Pakistan GPA & CGPA Calculator
A free, beautiful, and fully functional GPA & CGPA Calculator for all Pakistani university students. No signup required — just open and calculate!
🌐 Live Site: pakistangpacalulator.netlify.app

✨ Features

✅ Supports 80+ Pakistani Universities (KPK, Punjab, Sindh, Balochistan)
✅ Calculate Semester GPA instantly
✅ Calculate Cumulative CGPA with previous records
✅ Bar Chart — Grade points per subject
✅ Doughnut Chart — Grade distribution
✅ Print / Save as PDF — clean formatted result
✅ Animated result cards with letter grade & progress bar
✅ Fully responsive — works on mobile & desktop
✅ No backend, no database — runs 100% in the browser
✅ Completely free & open source


📸 Preview

Select your university → Enter grades & credit hours → Hit Calculate → Get GPA, CGPA, and visual charts instantly!

🚀 How to Use

Select your University and Semester
Enter your Subject Names, Grades, and Credit Hours
(Optional) Enter your Current CGPA and Completed Credit Hours for CGPA calculation
Click ⚡ Calculate GPA & CGPA
View results, charts, and summary
Click 🖨️ Print Results to save as PDF


🛠️ Tech Stack

HTML5 — Structure
CSS3 — Styling (no frameworks, pure CSS)
Vanilla JavaScript — Logic & calculations
Chart.js — Bar & Doughnut charts
Google Fonts — Playfair Display + DM Sans
